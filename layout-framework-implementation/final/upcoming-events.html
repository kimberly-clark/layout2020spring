Games and Competitions

Special Olympics Minnesota offers 17 Olympic-type sports for athletes of all abilities who are ages eight or older . These sports are divided into sports seasons and athletes compete in a variety of sports at the area, regional and state levels during each season. Special Olympics also provides athletes the opportunity to compete at a national and world level. All competitions are conducted according to the official Special Olympics Rules.

Fundraisers

Polar Plunge

Early each year, brave Plungers jump into frozen lakes across Minnesota to raise funds for SOMN. With over 20 locations across the state, there’s always a Plunge near you!

Unified Night Out

Unified Night Out (UNO) is a Unified party and fundraiser for Special Olympics Minnesota. Join the party for dancing, games, prizes and rides!

Plane Pull

The Plane Pull raises funds and awareness for more than 8,200 SOMN athletes. Don’t miss the fun as teams of up to 8 people compete to pull a Delta jet a distance of 25 feet across the tarmac in the fastest time!

Northland 300

The Northland 300 is a three-day 300-mile snowmobile ride made up of participants who raise funds to support SOMN.

Truck Convoy

The Truck Convoy is a two-day celebration that helps raise money and awareness for SOMN athletes. Truckers simply donate a $100 entry fee and convoy along a pre-determined route.
